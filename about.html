About me

I'm Eric Holzer, also known as erzloh. I'm a 23-year-old Swiss-Japanese Fullstack Web Developer passionate about beautiful and intentional design both technically and visually.

I'm studying Computer Science at École 42, where I've learned among others, C, C++, web technologies, networking, DevOps etc, along with teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills.

Alongside coding, I'm a Music Composer. I've written original pieces for brands, animations, games, etc, as well as personal projects like piano works and lo-fi albums.

Furthermore, I'm passionate about languages. I'm a native French speaker, fluent in English, and continuously and earnestly improving my Japanese. I'm somewhat alright in German, though I'd definitely love to take some time to brush it up!

I also run a YouTube channel and an Instagram account where I share things I'm interested in and where I document my learning journey.

At the heart of my work and life lie minimalism, beauty, and intentionality.
